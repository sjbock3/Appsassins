Technical Documentation

What Are We Building?

We sought to streamline the age old game of Assassins, a fancy game of tag.

Who Are We Building It For?

Our target audience is high school students, college students, and business interns that want to bond over a game of tag.

What Features Did We Want To Implement?

Our biggest features were our integration with Google Maps, the ability to create and host a game, and the ability to invite friends through email.

How Is The Project Implemented Technically?

Using a LAMP stack, Slim framework, and the concept behind a RESTful service to design Appsassins.

What Technologies Are Used?

Since we are using a LAMP stack, we are using Linux as our operating system, Apache as our host, MySQL for our database, and PHP as our scripting language.
