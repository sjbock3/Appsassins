Appsassins was created by the group of us developers who have all enjoyed playing the game of assassins. Although we all had a great time playing the classic version of assassins, we noticed a couple major flaws. First, the gamemaster has an extremely tough job keeping track of the players. Second, there are often conflicts about whether or not a player was actually tagged. We decided to create an Android and Web-based application to assist the gamemaker and the players to help promote a better, smoother playing experience of the classic game assassins
